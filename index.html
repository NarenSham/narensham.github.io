My Journey in Data & Analytics Engineering

Commercial Data Enablement Manager

Molson Coors Beverage Company | Toronto, ON
September 2023 - Present

At Molson Coors, I’ve been helping connect the dots between sales, planning, and commercial operations — building the systems that let data guide everyday business decisions.

  • Unified Data Architecture: I designed a unified data foundation that brought together market, POS, and performance data into one consistent ecosystem, giving commercial teams a clearer lens on how their decisions play out in the market.
  • Sales Planning & Revenue Enablement: Partnered with planning and finance to reshape how revenue insights flow through the organization, helping teams focus less on reconciling data and more on responding to it.
  • Supply Chain Visibility: Worked closely with supply planners to surface inventory signals earlier, creating smoother collaboration between customer operations and distribution partners.
  • Pricing & Promotions Strategy: Supported the commercial and category teams in building frameworks that combine pricing elasticity insights with market intuition, helping them plan smarter promotions and channel strategies.
  • Category Insights Platform: Helped design a data product that allows our category and sales teams to quickly assess product placement effectiveness, translating shelf performance into actionable insights.

Senior Consultant

Deloitte | Toronto, ON
November 2021 - June 2023

At Deloitte, I learned how to weave together business context and technical depth — helping some of the largest retail and CPG organizations rethink how they plan, forecast, and operate.

  • Planning Assessments: Worked with clients to map out how their planning processes connected across supply, finance, and merchandising, and identified where digital transformation could truly make a difference.
  • Solution Architecture: Designed technical blueprints for connected planning systems that aligned business functions and enabled faster, more reliable decision-making.
  • Prototyping & Demos: Brought ideas to life through functional demos and prototypes, helping clients visualize the end-state and gain confidence in their transformation journey.
  • Integration Design: Helped design integrations between core systems like SAP, Oracle, and planning tools — creating smoother data flow across planning, pricing, and product lifecycle operations.

Consultant
